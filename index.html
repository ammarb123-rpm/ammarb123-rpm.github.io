We are getting married

The date is the 28th of June 2025 and we would like you to be a part of it.

Saturday, the 28th of June, 2025

Ceremony

Ceremony 4PM - 6PM

The ceremony will be held outdoors, weather permitting. In the event of rain, we will move the celebration indoors. The ceremony is set to last approximately one hour, during which Ammar and Ella will exchange vows and make a promise to love and cherish each other for the rest of their lives. This heartfelt occasion will be a beautiful celebration of their love, surrounded by family and friends.

Cocktail Time 6PM - 7PM

The cocktail hour will be held outdoors, allowing guests to enjoy the fresh air and stunning surroundings, weather permitting. In the event of rain, we will relocate to a cozy indoor space. This is a wonderful opportunity to mingle, savor delicious appetizers, and sip on refreshing beverages as we celebrate Ammar and Ella’s special day.

Dinner & Celebration

Welcome & Toasts 7PM-8PM

As the festivities continue, we invite all guests to take their seats and prepare for a warm welcome. Ammar and Ella will be introduced to their loved ones for the first time as a married couple. Heartfelt toasts will be made by family and friends, sharing words of love, laughter, and cherished memories. There will be a little dancing to set the mood for the evening ahead!

Feast & Festivities 8PM-3AM

Prepare for an unforgettable evening filled with delicious food and lively dancing! As we gather around the tables, guests will enjoy a carefully curated menu featuring a selection of delectable dishes, designed to please every palate. Once dinner is complete, the dance floor will come alive!
